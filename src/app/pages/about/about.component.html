Our Origin Story

From Egyptian Vision to Global Innovation

Founded in Cairo in 2023, NanoCare emerged from a simple observation: 90% of traditional supplements were being destroyed by stomach acid before reaching their target cells. Our Egyptian founders, combining backgrounds in pharmaceuticals and nanotechnology, asked one revolutionary question:

"What if we could protect nutrients all the way to the cellular level, ensuring every gram delivers maximum impact?"

Today, NanoCare stands as Egypt's first and leading liposomal supplement company, bridging cutting-edge nanotechnology with centuries of Egyptian wellness wisdom.
